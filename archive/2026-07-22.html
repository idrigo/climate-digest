Stretched-Grid vs. Limited-Area Models for Data-Driven Regional Weather Forecasting

Wijnands et al. · Environmental Modelling & Software · arXiv:2507.18378 · 2026

Using ECMWF's Anemoi framework, researchers systematically compare limited-area models (LAM, which require lateral boundary forcing from an external global model) and stretched-grid models (SGM, global domain with enhanced resolution over the region of interest) for deterministic high-resolution forecasting over Europe; SGM better preserves large-scale dynamics while LAM achieves finer regional detail, with results pointing toward hybrid architectures as the practical path forward for next-generation regional machine learning weather prediction.

Систематическое сравнение LAM и SGM для детерминированного AI-прогноза над Европой (Anemoi/ECMWF): SGM лучше сохраняет крупномасштабную динамику, LAM даёт более высокое региональное разрешение; оптимальным признан гибридный подход.
