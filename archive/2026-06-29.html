New Partnership with Resilens AG

Reliable climate data is becoming an essential component of long-term planning. Whether cities are assessing future heat exposure or organisations are evaluating infrastructure risks, informed decisions begin with accurate data. To strengthen this process, meteoblue has entered a strategic partnership with Resilens AG, a company specialising in climate risk assessment and adaptation planning. Through this collaboration, high-resolution meteoblue climate data will be integrated into the Resilens platform, supporting vulnerability analyses, exposure assessments and the prioritisation of climate adaptation measures.
