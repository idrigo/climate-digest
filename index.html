Limnology & Oceanography / Scripps 19 JUL
Aquatic Deoxygenation Should Become the 10th Planetary Boundary, Scripps Study Argues

Ferrer et al. · Limnology and Oceanography · DOI: 10.1002/lno.70434 · Scripps / UC San Diego · 2026

A review paper in Limnology and Oceanography led by Erica Ferrer (Scripps Institution of Oceanography, UC San Diego) argues that the rapid loss of dissolved oxygen from oceans, coastal waters, rivers, and lakes — driven by warming, agricultural nutrient runoff, and slowdown in deep-water ventilation — satisfies the criteria for a tenth planetary boundary. Current oxygen depletion trajectories across multiple ocean basins are already in the framework's "unsafe" zone, with cascading consequences that include weakening of the biological carbon pump (reducing ocean CO₂ uptake), expansion of dead zones, collapse of fisheries, and amplified nutrient cycling feedbacks; recovery from deep-basin deoxygenation spans centuries to millennia. The authors call for treating aquatic oxygen loss with the same policy urgency as atmospheric CO₂.

Обзор Ferrer et al. (Scripps/UCSD) в Limnology and Oceanography: потеря растворённого O₂ из океанов, рек и озёр вследствие потепления, стока удобрений и замедления вентиляции глубин соответствует критериям десятой планетарной границы. Текущие траектории — уже в «опасной» зоне: ослабление биологической углеродной помпы, расширение мёртвых зон, обрушение рыбных запасов. Восстановление глубинной деоксигенации занимает века–тысячелетия.

arXiv (NVIDIA / LBNL / PNNL / LLNL) 30 JUN
STRATA: First Global Storm-Resolving AI Emulator at 4.9 km Resolution — 50× More Efficient Than Physics Models

NVIDIA, Lawrence Berkeley NL, Pacific Northwest NL, Lawrence Livermore NL · arXiv:2606.31248 · physics.ao-ph · 2026

Researchers from NVIDIA, LBNL, PNNL, and LLNL introduce STRATA (Storm-resolving Tile-based autoRegressive Atmosphere Transformer Architecture), the first autoregressive AI emulator for global storm-resolving atmospheric dynamics. Trained on only 17 days of SCREAM physics-model output at 4.9-km resolution (~25 million grid cells, 10-minute samples), STRATA achieves stable 24-hour global rollouts with realistic km-scale convective dynamics — expanding prior AI state-of-the-art from 900 km domain / 2-hour rollouts to full-planet / 24-hour rollouts. Energy efficiency is approximately 50× over the physics model (roughly 1/50 MWh per simulated day at H100 GPU scale). The model uses a novel Stereographic Rotary Position Embedding (StereoRoPE) for grid-invariant encoding; large-scale biases accumulate at extended lead times, but the work opens a practical pathway to convection-resolving planetary simulation.

NVIDIA + национальные лаборатории США (LBNL, PNNL, LLNL) представляют STRATA — первый авторегрессионный AI-эмулятор глобальной шторм-разрешающей динамики атмосферы. Обучен на 17 сутках выхода SCREAM при 4,9 км, 10-минутная дискретизация; стабильные 24-часовые глобальные прогоны с реалистичной конвективной динамикой; 50× экономия энергии по сравнению с физической моделью. Первый прорыв к конвективному прогнозированию в планетарном масштабе.

Abundant Feedbacks Between Aquatic Deoxygenation and Planetary Boundaries Point to "Unsafe" Oxygen Loss

Ferrer et al. · Limnology and Oceanography · DOI: 10.1002/lno.70434 · Scripps Institution of Oceanography / UC San Diego · 2026

A comprehensive review in Limnology and Oceanography by Erica Ferrer and colleagues at Scripps/UC San Diego identifies abundant interactions between aquatic deoxygenation and all nine existing planetary boundaries — from climate change to biosphere integrity to freshwater flows — arguing that oxygen loss from oceans, coastal waters, rivers, and lakes satisfies the criteria for a tenth planetary boundary. The authors call for treating aquatic deoxygenation with the same policy urgency as atmospheric CO₂ or biodiversity loss.

A comprehensive H1 2026 analysis by CTVC (Currence) shows global climate tech venture investment rose 55% year-on-year to $26.1 billion in the first half of 2026 — the strongest first half since 2022 — even as deal count fell 25%, indicating capital is concentrating in larger rounds. Low-carbon data center infrastructure accounted for 34% of all climate tech funding, up from just 3% a year earlier, with the built environment vertical growing more than 800% and surpassing energy as the single largest funding category. Carbon-focused startups had their weakest half since 2020 (funding down 61%) as US subsidies faded; physical climate risk and adaptation tools attracted growing growth-stage investment as extreme weather events turned actuarial risk into immediate business cost. Fervo Energy's $1.9 billion geothermal IPO and X-Energy's $1 billion nuclear IPO led the sector's public market activity.

По данным CTVC (Currence), климат-тек VC вырос на 55% до $26,1 млрд в H1 2026 — лучший первый квартал с 2022 года — при снижении числа сделок на 25%. Низкоуглеродные дата-центры: 34% всего финансирования (3% год назад), рост сектора «строительная среда» — 800%. Углеродные стартапы: минимум с 2020 года (−61%). IPO года — Fervo Energy ($1,9 млрд, геотермальная энергетика) и X-Energy ($1 млрд, ядерная).
